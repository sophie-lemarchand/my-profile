Experience

From one thing to another, I got closer to the textile industry. Indeed, with all the current challenges on pollution in this industry, I went to visit the countries and factories producing textiles for the companies in which I worked. It made me realize that I wanted to contribute to the evolution of society towards a more sustainable world. So I joined the De Marzi company, whose values ​​I share. Today we build luxurious pieces with respect for the environment. I am proud of the path I have taken and wish to influence other companies in this field.
